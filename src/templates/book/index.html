Overview

Clever, stylish solutions for today’s travellers Catering to transiting international and domestic passengers, Holiday Inn Express International Airport is conveniently located within the airport’s sleek new Terminal. Featuring all the comforts Holiday Inn Express International Airport guests have come to expect, the hotel’s clever design and signature ‘can do’ service puts the pleasure back into travelling, making tedious layovers a distant memory.

Airport Hotel Rooms

Offering a hybrid of services for the self-sufficient traveller, all rooms come with high speed internet access, LCD TV with in room entertainment. Constantly striving to find innovative solutions for the modern traveller’s needs, the hotel also offers seven Aqua Pods. These private shower rooms allow passengers looking for a pre or post-flight freshen up, or those with shorter layovers, to relax and enjoy the hotel’s facilities.

Dining

Located on the lobby levels of both the International and Domestic wings, the T Lounge serves an all-day menu of freshly prepared favourites. From light snacks and teas, to comfort food and cocktails, the T Lounge has something to suit every taste.

Facilities

The hotel features a fully equipped Exert Fitness Centre, a rejuvenating Essentials Spa, and E Point which allows guests Internet access, a computer and a printer at any time of the day or night.
